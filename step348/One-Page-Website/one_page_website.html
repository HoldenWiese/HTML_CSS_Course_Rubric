The Rocks of Rock Climbing

Here are a few different kinds of rock types that climbers like.

Rock type: Granite
Visual characteristics: Granite is beautiful and exposed. Granite faces are some of the largest in the world.
Climbing characteristics: Granite climbing is lots of crack climbing and trad climbing. It is also a favorite for slab climbers.

Rock type: Sandstone
Visual characteristics: Many beautiful colors.
Climbing characteristics: Many types of climbing styles are found on sandstone. Crack climbing and well defined sport routes are most available.

Rock type: Limestone
Visual characteristics: Sharp and defined rock faces and ranges.
Climbing characteristics: Sharp holds are readily available making limestone a favorite for sport climbers.
